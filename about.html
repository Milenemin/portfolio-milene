About

My name is Milene, and I study Multimedia Design at EK. I have a strong interest in visual communication and in creating coherent, aesthetically refined designs and patterns.

Research forms the foundation of my work. I value user-centered design and believe strong design and UX must work hand in hand to create intuitive and engaging solutions. Through interviews and observation, I translate user insights into thoughtful design decisions.

Alongside my studies, I work as a yoga teacher and acupuncturist, where years of working closely with people have sharpened my sensitivity to nuance and individual needs — something that strongly informs my design process.
I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Humanities (Cultural Studies and English), which strengthened my analytical thinking and communication skills.

In 2025, I worked as a Social Media Coordinator for a tango school, creating content that significantly increased both engagement and follower growth.

I am a native English and French speaker and also speak fluent Danish and Spanish.
